Strong Curriculum, Strong in faith About Our Center

Founded in 1987,The Northland Church of Christ Child Care provides learning experiences with Christian emphasis. Biblical teachings as well as academic lessons are part of the daily schedule. Children from 6 weeks old through 5 years old attend. Christian based curriculums are used in the pre-school classes and workbooks are provided. The child care is open all year from 6:30 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. Our rates are very reasonable and Title XX is accepted. We have experienced and caring teachers. Nearly half of our staff has been with us for over ten years. Parent visits are welcomed.

We guarantee that you will fall in love with our center to the moment you walk in.
